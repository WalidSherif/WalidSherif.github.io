## Project Summary

This project used analytical airfoil generation and computational flow analysis to study aerodynamic behavior around a Joukowski profile.

## Scope

- Geometry generation through conformal mapping.
- Flow-field visualization and interpretation.
- Pressure-distribution analysis.
- Lift and moment characterization across angle of attack.

## Outcome

The report demonstrates a strong foundation in aerodynamic modeling, flow interpretation, and technical presentation.
