## Project Summary

This report focused on classical-control design for aircraft longitudinal pitch dynamics with emphasis on stability and compensator tuning.

## Scope

- Transfer-function based system modeling.
- Open-loop stability analysis.
- PID-controller synthesis and tuning.
- Performance comparison in simulation.

## Outcome

The project strengthened analytical understanding of classical feedback design for aerospace-related dynamics.
