## Project Summary

This project examined the technical and economic trade-offs involved in large-scale transmission-line design.

## Scope

- Candidate-voltage comparison.
- Line-parameter and bundle calculations.
- Regulation and efficiency analysis.
- Design optimization under technical constraints.

## Outcome

The study highlights quantitative engineering comparison and MATLAB-based decision support for power-system design.
